INTRODUCTION TO PROBABILITY

Definition

Probability is a measure of the likelihood that an event will occur, expressed as a number between 0 and 1. An event with a probability of 1 is certain to occur, while an event with a probability of 0 is impossible.

Probability is a number between 0 and 1 that describes the chance that a stated event will occur.

Formulas

  • Probability of an event (P): P = Number of favorable outcomes / Total number of outcomes
  • Joint probability: P(A and B) = P(A) * P(B given A)
  • Conditional probability: P(A given B) = P(A and B) / P(B)

Examples

  • The probability of flipping a coin and getting heads is 1/2.
  • The probability of rolling a die and getting a 4 is 1/6.
  • The probability of drawing an ace from a deck of cards is 4/52.
